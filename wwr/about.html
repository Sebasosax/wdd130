Down the River

Our purpose at Down the River is to inspire adventure and foster a deep connection with nature through thrilling and safe rafting experiences in Costa Rica.

Our mission is to provide exceptional service, expert guides, and unforgettable trips.

We believe in respecting the environment, committing to safety, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

Our motto is “Pura vida and pura adventure in every rapid!”

History

Down the River was founded in 2010 by a group of friends passionate about nature and rafting in Costa Rica who wanted to share their passion with more people. Their adventure began by exploring lesser-known rivers, and over time, they perfected their routes and services to provide the best possible experience. Thanks to the dedication of a committed team and expert guides, the company has steadily grown, becoming a trusted choice for those seeking to experience the thrill of rafting and connect with nature in a safe and responsible way. We offer a wide variety of rafting activities, supported by staff who are dedicated to their work and genuinely care about their customers, ensuring they have a fun time, stay safe, and learn from this unique experience that Down the River can provide.
